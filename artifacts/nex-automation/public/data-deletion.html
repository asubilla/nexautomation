Data Deletion Request

Request complete removal of all your data from our systems

📅 Last updated: June 2025
ℹ️

This page is provided in compliance with Meta's Platform Terms (Facebook & Instagram) and our Privacy Policy. You have the right to request deletion of all data associated with your account at any time.

What Data We Hold

Data TypeDescriptionStatus After Deletion
Platform Credentials Encrypted username/password for TikTok, Instagram, Facebook Permanently Deleted
Upload History Records of videos uploaded and to which platforms Permanently Deleted
Browser Sessions Saved login sessions (cookies) for automation Permanently Deleted
Server Logs Access logs containing your IP address Purged within 30 days

How to Request Deletion

  1. Option 1 – Via the App (Instant)

    Log into Nex Automation → Credentials page → Click "Delete" on each connected account. All credential data is removed immediately from the database.

  2. Option 2 – Email Request

    Send an email to asubilla115@gmail.com with subject: "Data Deletion Request". Include the email or username associated with your account. We will process within 72 hours.

  3. Option 3 – Contact Form

    Fill out the Contact Us form below, selecting "Data Deletion Request" as the subject. We will confirm deletion via email.

Facebook / Instagram Data Deletion (Meta)

If you connected your Facebook or Instagram account to Nex Automation and wish to revoke access and delete all associated data:

  1. Remove App Access from Facebook

    Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → Find "Nex Automation" → Click "Remove".

  2. Submit Deletion Request Here

    After removing app access, submit a deletion request using any option above. We will delete all associated data from our systems within 72 hours.

  3. Confirmation

    You will receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete. No data will be retained after this point.

Quick Deletion Request

✅ Deletion request received. We will process it within 72 hours and send confirmation to your email.

Retention After Deletion

After a deletion request is processed:

  • All credentials are immediately removed from our encrypted database
  • Upload history is permanently deleted
  • Session cookies are cleared
  • Server access logs are purged within 30 days (standard log rotation)
  • No backups containing your data are retained
