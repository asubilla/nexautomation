Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

📅 Last updated: June 2025
Summary: Nex Automation stores your social media credentials (encrypted) and video upload history only for the purpose of automating video cross-posting. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

1. Who We Are

Nex Automation ("we", "our", or "us") is a social media automation tool that connects your YouTube channel to TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook, automatically uploading new videos to these platforms on your behalf.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: asubilla115@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Credentials

To automate uploads on your behalf, we collect and securely store:

  • TikTok username and password (AES-256 encrypted at rest)
  • Instagram username and password (AES-256 encrypted at rest)
  • Facebook email/username and password (AES-256 encrypted at rest)
  • YouTube channel URL (public information, used for monitoring)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Upload job history (which videos were posted, when, and to which platforms)
  • Error logs for failed upload attempts
  • Basic server access logs (IP address, timestamp, HTTP method)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not read your private messages on any platform
  • We do not access your followers, following lists, or DMs
  • We do not store video content — videos are downloaded temporarily and deleted after upload

3. How We Use Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Data

When you connect your Facebook or Instagram account, we use your credentials exclusively to:

  • Log into your account via automated browser (Playwright) to upload videos
  • Verify that the credentials are valid before saving them

We do not use Meta data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than uploading videos you explicitly configure.

We do not share Meta user data with any third parties.

4. How We Use TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we use your credentials exclusively to:

  • Log into TikTok via automated browser to upload videos on your behalf
  • Verify credential validity at the time of saving

We do not read your TikTok feed, comments, messages, or analytics. We do not share TikTok data with third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Credentials are stored in a PostgreSQL database with AES-256 encryption
  • Database is hosted on Neon.tech with SSL-enforced connections
  • Server access is limited to authorized personnel only
  • No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Account credentialsUntil you delete the account or request deletion
Upload job history90 days, then automatically purged
Temporary video filesDeleted immediately after upload
Server access logs30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct: Update any inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of all your data — see our Data Deletion page
  • Portability: Request your upload history in JSON format
  • Withdraw consent: Disconnect any platform at any time from the app

8. Third-Party Services

We interact with the following third-party platforms on your behalf:

  • TikTok – videos are uploaded to tiktok.com using your credentials
  • Instagram / Facebook – videos are uploaded via instagram.com and facebook.com
  • YouTube – public video data is fetched from your channel page (no authentication required)
  • Neon.tech – PostgreSQL database hosting. See Neon Privacy Policy

9. Children's Privacy

Nex Automation is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
